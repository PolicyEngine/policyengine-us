## Parameters

### `smi_limit`
*LIHEAP state median income limit*

The US Department of Health and Human Services requires states to provide assistance only to households with income below this percentage of the state median income through the Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program.

**Unit: /1 | Period: year**

Current value (2021-01-01): **0.6**
