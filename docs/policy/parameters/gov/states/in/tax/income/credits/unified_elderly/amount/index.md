## Parameters

### `one_aged`
*Indiana unified elderly tax credit amount for one aged*

Indiana provides the following amounts under the unified elderly tax credit for filing units containing just one aged head or spouse.

**Type: single_amount**


### `two_aged`
*Indiana unified elderly tax credit amount for two aged*

Indiana provides the following amounts under the unified elderly tax credit for filing units containing both an aged head and an aged spouse.

**Type: single_amount**
