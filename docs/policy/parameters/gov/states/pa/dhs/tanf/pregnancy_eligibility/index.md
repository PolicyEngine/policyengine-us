## Parameters

### `age_limit`
*Pennsylvania TANF age limitation for pregnant women with child receiving TANF*

Pennsylvania has limitation for applying TANF to pregnant women above or equal to this age.

**Unit: year**

Current value (2013-08-16): **18**
