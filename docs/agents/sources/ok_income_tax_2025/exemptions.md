# Oklahoma 2025 Exemptions

**Source**: Form 511-NR-Pkt, Pages 9-10
**URL**: https://oklahoma.gov/content/dam/ok/en/tax/documents/forms/individuals/current/511-NR-Pkt.pdf

## Personal Exemption Amount

**Amount**: $1,000 per person

Each taxpayer and qualifying dependent is eligible for a $1,000 exemption.

**Source**: Page 10

## Special Exemption for Blind/65+

Taxpayers who are 65 or older or who are blind may claim an additional special exemption.

### AGI Limits for Special Exemption (Page 9)

| Filing Status | Maximum Federal AGI |
|---------------|---------------------|
| Single | $15,000 |
| Married Filing Jointly | $25,000 |
| Married Filing Separately | $12,500 |
| Head of Household | $19,000 |
| Qualifying Surviving Spouse | $25,000 |

### Special Exemption Requirements

- Must be 65 or older OR legally blind
- Federal AGI must not exceed the limit for filing status
- Amount: $1,000 per qualifying condition (can claim both age and blindness)

## Values Unchanged

Exemption amounts have remained unchanged since 2021 and continue through 2025.
