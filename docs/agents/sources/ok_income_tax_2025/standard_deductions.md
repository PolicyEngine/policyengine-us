# Oklahoma 2025 Standard Deductions

**Source**: Form 511-NR-Pkt, Page 10
**URL**: https://oklahoma.gov/content/dam/ok/en/tax/documents/forms/individuals/current/511-NR-Pkt.pdf#page=10

## Standard Deduction Amounts (Tax Year 2025)

| Filing Status | Standard Deduction |
|---------------|-------------------|
| Single | $6,350 |
| Married Filing Jointly | $12,700 |
| Married Filing Separately | $6,350 |
| Head of Household | $9,350 |
| Qualifying Surviving Spouse | $12,700 |

## Rules

1. If you claimed the standard deduction on your federal return, you must claim the Oklahoma standard deduction.

2. If you claimed itemized deductions on your federal return, you must claim Oklahoma itemized deductions.

3. You qualify for the Oklahoma standard deduction even when claimed as a dependent on another return.

## Values Unchanged

The standard deduction amounts have remained unchanged since 2021 and continue through 2025.
